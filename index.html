Your projects, env contracts, and encrypted secrets on every machine. One command onboards a new laptop, VM, or teammate: repos arrive, env files land, secrets stay sealed until a device you approve needs them. Works offline, self-host or cloud.

Real block previews for Umbraco

Umbraco's block editors show gray cards where the page should be. Ripple Preview renders every block through your site's own views, styles and scripts, so editors see the real page while they edit it. Free and open source.
