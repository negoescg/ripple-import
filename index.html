Features

Everything you need to import data

Built for speed, accuracy, and zero frustration.

CSV & Excel

Drag & drop CSV, XLSX, or multi-sheet workbooks. Your data is parsed instantly in the browser.

Smart column mapping

Columns are auto-matched to your board by name and type. Adjust, skip, or create new columns on the fly.

Items & subitems

Import parent items with subitems. Hierarchy is auto-detected from type columns, indentation, or references.

Multi-level boards

Full support for multi-level boards with up to 5 levels of nesting. Add children to any item at any depth.

Duplicate detection

Skip items that already exist on your board. No accidental duplicates, no manual cleanup.

Templates

Download a CSV or Excel template that matches your board's columns. Fill it in, upload, done.
