Secrets never travel in plaintext

Most sync tools treat secrets like files. RippleRoot treats them like secrets: encrypted on your machine, sealed in transit, and released only to devices you approve — for as long as you allow.

  • age x25519 encryption at rest; plaintext never touches the server
  • New devices need an explicit approval before any pull
  • Short-lived leases expire on their own — nothing lingers
